



Electrical Connections

Electrical Outlets
 - Electrical Boxes will be metal.
 - Electrical boxes will be sealed with Sound and Fire Rated Acoustical Putty Pads.
 - Outlets in all areas will be of commercial grade.
 - Outlets and switches will be wired using screw-clamp connectors.
 - Outlets and switches will be wrapped in electrical tape appropriate for the current. (i.e. Super 88, 3m 35, etc)
 - Outlets and switches should be connected via pigtail utilizing Wago 221 switches to maximize box space.
