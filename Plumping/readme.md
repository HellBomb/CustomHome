Plumbing design
    Hybrid manifold system. Each room with water access should have its own dedicated hot, cold, and recirculation lines with mini-manifold systems at the individual room and should be hidden yet accessible. No fitting should be inaccessible for both maintainence and inspection purposes. 
    

**Water Heater**

    Propane hybrid tank/tankless water heater 
   
**Incoming Water Line**

   Install pressure regulator at the point where the municipal water enters the house
  
  
**Incoming Water Source**

    All water lines should make use of water hammer arrestors to prevent water hammer from causing small damage over time.

**Plumbing drainage Water Line**

   All non-toilet drainage valves should utilize P-Traps with cleanout, and should utilize either a Solids Interceptor or in-line filter in combination with basket strainer.
    
**Sewer line**

    Sewer line should have check valve to prevent backflow.
