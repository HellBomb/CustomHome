Plumbing design
    Hybrid manifold system. Each room with water access should have its own dedicated hot, cold, and recirculation lines with mini-manifold systems at the individual room and should be hidden yet accessible. No fitting should be inaccessible for both maintainence and inspection purposes. 
    

**Water Heater**
    Propane hybrid tank/tankless water heater
